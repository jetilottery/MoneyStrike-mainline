Money Strike

It is an explosion of riches in Money Strike! Reveal Lucky Numbers in the money bags and match them to Your Numbers in the stacks of cash and win a prize. Keep an eye out for Gold Bars – revealing them awards an instant prize! Have a blast when you reveal the cash!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Select symbols to reveal numbers. Match any “Your Number to any Winning Number to win a prize. Reveal a Gold Bar symbol to win a prize instantly.

After all numbers are revealed, a closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press AUTO REVEAL to have the game play to completion automatically. Game play con-tinues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
